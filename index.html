How to Play

Ember — A Game for Two
1
Set up your game
Enter your names, pick your moods, choose your love languages, set a heat level and a points target. These shape the cards you receive all evening.
2
Take turns drawing cards
Roll the dice for a random card, choose a level directly, or enter Truth · Dare · Command. You alternate turns after every card.
3
Complete challenges, earn points
Tap Done to earn your points. Tap Skip to pass — no points awarded. Harder cards and timed cards earn more. Heart a card to save your favourites.
4
Watch for special cards
✦ Ember Moments appear rarely and glow gold — they are the most intimate cards in the deck. 💬 Check In cards appear periodically — pause and be honest with each other.
5
First to the target wins
When a player hits the target a Winner screen appears, the winner claims their reward, and your evening's Afterglow summary is revealed.
